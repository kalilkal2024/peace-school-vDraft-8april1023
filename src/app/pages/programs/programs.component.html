Our Programs

Discover our comprehensive range of educational programs designed to empower students and foster academic excellence.

Language Programs

English Program

Our English Program offers comprehensive language instruction from beginner to advanced levels. Classes focus on all language skills including speaking, listening, reading, and writing.

Duration: 12 weeks per level
Schedule: Morning and evening classes
Requirements:
  • Placement test
  • Learning materials
  • Regular attendance

Khmer Language Program

Immerse yourself in Cambodian culture while learning the Khmer language. Our program combines language instruction with cultural activities.

Duration: 10 weeks per level
Schedule: Flexible class times
Requirements:
  • No prior knowledge required
  • Learning materials
  • Cultural activities participation

Academic Programs

Mathematics Program

Strong foundation in mathematical concepts through interactive learning methods. Develop problem-solving skills and logical thinking abilities.

Duration: 16 weeks per semester
Schedule: Twice weekly classes

Social Studies Program

Explore various aspects of society, history, and cultural perspectives. Develop critical thinking skills while understanding different social contexts.

Duration: 1 semester
Schedule: Weekly classes

Social Science Program

Focus on understanding human behavior and social relationships through scientific methods. Learn research methodologies and conduct social science projects.

Duration: 1 semester
Schedule: Weekly lectures and lab sessions

Special Programs

Student Exchange Program

Experience cultural exchange with international students while studying abroad at one of our partner institutions worldwide.

Duration: 1 semester to 1 year
Requirements:
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Language proficiency
  • Letter of recommendation

Scholarship Program

Financial assistance for academically outstanding students who demonstrate financial need. Covers tuition fees and educational materials.

Duration: Full academic year
Requirements:
  • Academic excellence
  • Financial need documentation
  • Personal statement
